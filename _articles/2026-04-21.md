> **Why does queue age matter if the diffs are machine-validated?**
> Because stale PRs accumulate merge conflicts as the base branch moves. A PR that was conflict-free at day 1 may require a rebase at day 7. The Opus validation is against the branch at open time — a conflict-rebase introduces new diffs that are not re-validated. The longer the queue sits, the higher the probability that merging the oldest PR requires manual conflict resolution, negating the automation benefit entirely.
